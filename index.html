A game for two players!

Groundmole Antonio wants to grow so high that he can take over the world!
Gardener Louis notices the danger and tries to thwart the evil groundmole plans!

Antonio grows with A button.
Louis takes Antonio back to ground with L button.

It is recommended to do whatever it takes to keep the second player away from the keyboard in order to win :) (just keep it legal!)

Made in 6h for Ludum Dare 34 by dos dosowski
using allegro.js by Sos Sosowski
